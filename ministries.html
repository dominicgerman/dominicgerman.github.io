Our ministries

Student
Ministry

Our student ministry (grades 5th-12th) is an inclusive environment where students can express themselves, build lasting friendships, and feel supported on their journey of faith. In between Six Flags trips and Braves games, the ministry provides opportunities for leadership training, community service projects, and outreach initiatives. The welcoming environment, engaging activities, and transformative encounters with Jesus empower young hearts to thrive in their faith journey and impact the world around them.

Children's
Ministry

Clearview creates a safe, nurturing environment for kids, where they can grow, explore, and feel loved. Our dedicated volunteers provide a fun-filled atmosphere with engaging activities and interactive games. Amidst laughter and play, we never lose sight of our core purpose: teaching children about Jesus. Through age-appropriate lessons and hands-on experiences, they discover His love, forgiveness, and acceptance. This holistic approach fosters a strong faith foundation and lifelong relationship with Him.

College

Clearview hosts a vibrant college-age ministry that provides a transformative community for young adults. By integrating faith into their real-life issues, students develop a mature relationship with Jesus. As a result, young adults can thrive in their faith, impacting their campus and beyond.

Sunday School /   Life Group

Clearview’s Sunday School Life Groups provide a transformative and intimate setting for scripture study and meaningful connections. Participants engage in discussions, explore Bible teachings, and share their journeys within a safe community. Through practical biblical principles, valuable insights are gained for daily life. Authentic friendships cultivate mutual accountability, prayer support, and personal growth. Sunday School Life Groups foster fellowship, warmth, and care. Engaging in Scripture study equips participants to navigate challenges with wisdom and faith. Clearview offers three adult classes based on life stages.

Worship

The Worship Ministry at Clearview is led by a team of dedicated musicians and singers who strive to create an atmosphere of praise and adoration in each service. Using a combination of modern worship songs and traditional hymns, they seek to engage the congregation in heartfelt worship. Despite the high level of musicianship maintained through regular rehearsals, the worship ministry remains a place for anyone with a passion to lead the church community into a deeper encounter with Jesus.

Missions

Clearview’s missions ministry is dedicated to making a positive impact in marginalized communities by demonstrating Christ's love through acts of kindness. Our teams have constructed homes and created lasting relationships in Costa Rica, Honduras, and Haiti to name a few. Clearview’s missions ministry is committed to spreading the gospel by providing practical help, and by embodying compassion and service.
