The main goal of our service is to connect people with God. Each and every service we show a passion for the Word, Worship, and Prayer. We hope to see you there!

The Word

A main part of our service is the Sermon. We believe that God speaks to His people through the Bible. Our messages focus on God's Word and how to apply it to our lives.

Worship

Our worship is designed to bring God glory through music. We typically have a blended service with both contemporary praise and worship as well as some of the classic hymns of the faith.

Prayer

Our church believes strongly in the power of prayer and we include special times of prayer in our service. We aim for people to connect with God in an authentic way.

What we believe

Our church is a non-denominational congregation that is affiliated with the Christian movement Church of God . This movement of Jesus followers has placed an emphasis on salvation, holiness, and unity. Anyone, regardless of their denominational background, who has accepted Christ as their savior is a "member" of God's universal church. All believers are brothers and sisters in Christ.

We hold basic orthodox Christian beliefs. In seeking to build harmony within Christian fellowships we often rely on this early church guidance, "in essentials unity, in non-essentials liberty, and in all things charity (or love)." Basically, we like to focus on the core elements of Christianity, while allowing room for other points of view in non-essential doctrines.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God. It is authoritative for both faith and practice. What the Bible teaches is binding on the lives of all. It is God's eternal truth.

God

We believe that God is infinite, all-knowing, and is worthy of our worship and obedience. He is perfect in all His attributes. God is one in essence, yet eternally existing in three Persons—Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Humanity

Humankind was made in the likeness and image of God. However, because of sin, humankind incurred the penalty of spiritual and physical death.

Salvation

The shed blood of Jesus Christ on the cross provides the only acceptable sacrifice for the payment of humanity's sin debt before a holy God. God made a way for our sins to be forgiven through the death of His only begotten Son.

The Christian Life

God wants all persons to have a real relationship with Him through His Son, Jesus Christ. The Christian life is one of surrender to God - life only begins to make sense when God is in His rightful place at the center of your heart.

The Church

The church is made up of those who have placed their faith in Jesus Christ for the forgiveness of their sins. It is a place where believers receive healing and grow in God's truth, and where they are equipped to impact the world with gospel.
